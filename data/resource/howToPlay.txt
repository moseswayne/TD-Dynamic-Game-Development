Hello and welcome to the “I Heart Singletons” – Duvall, probably help screen. To select and object to place, please (1) click on the wanted icon which will open a sliding pane. Then (2) click on the preferred actor which will appear on top of the other. Next, (3) click and drag the new icon onto the screen in an appropriate location. (4) Right click to lock the item in place on screen. (If the icon is relocatable, please pause game a remove, else the icon will be locked in place.)
